The Automotive Mission Plan 2016-26 (AMP 2026) is the collective vision of Government of India (Government) and the Indian Automotive
Industry on where the Vehicles, Autocomponents, and Tractor industries should reach over the next ten years in terms of size, contribution
to India’s development, global footprint, technological maturity, competitiveness, and institutional structure and capabilities. AMP
2026 also seeks to define the trajectory of evolution of the automotive ecosystem in India including the glide path of specific regulations
and policies that govern research, design, technology, testing, manufacturing, import/ export, sale, use, repair, and recycling of
automotive vehicles, components and services. AMP 2026 is a document that is aimed at multiple stakeholders in India and overseas, and seeks to
communicate the Government and industry’s intent and objectives pertaining to the Indian Automotive industry, comprising the automotive
vehicle manufacturers, the auto-component manufacturers and tractor manufacturers who operate in India.
