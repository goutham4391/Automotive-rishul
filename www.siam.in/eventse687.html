  • SAFE Annual Convention 2022 - (Safe Journey): Prioritising Imperatives of Road Safety and Environment

    Venue : Swosti Hotel, Bhubaneshwar Date : 30-Sep-2022

    As part of SIAM Sustainable Mobility Advocacy Concoction FY 2022-23, (Safe Journey) initiative has been approved to highlight contributions made by Indian auto industry in providing safer, affordable and environmentally benign inclusive mobility to all. In order to prioritise road safety namely environment, engineering, enforcement, education and emergency care, Society for Automotive Fitness & Environment (SAFE - An initiative of SIAM) has been engaged in doing pioneering work since year 1999. SAFE is dedicated towards engaging with road safety stakeholders namely Policy makers both at Centre & States, Enforcement authorities, Vehicle Manufacturers, Vehicle Owners, drivers, public including pedestrians, students, other road users, personnel involved in emergency care, etc. in its endeavor to spread 360 degree awareness on imperatives of safety on road.

  • 62nd SIAM Annual Convention 2022

    Venue : Hotel Taj Palace, New Delhi Date : 15-Sep-2022

    Automotive Industry is one of the main pillars of the Indian Economy. It has very strong backward & forward linkages and is a key driver for countrys growth. The Indian Automotive Industry contributes about 6 per cent to the countrys GDP, around 35 per cent to the Manufacturing GDP, supports over 4 million jobs directly and as many as 27 million more jobs indirectly. With the pro-active support from the Government of India, the Indian Automotive Industry has been able to withstand the severe impact of the Covid pandemic in the last two years. Government of Indias support has been phenomenal, especially through the introduction of FAME and PLI Schemes, as these are helping the Auto Industry to smoothly transit into the next realm of Advanced Automotive Technologies and become self-reliant. While the Industry is working hard to get back to the growth path, as being envisioned in the Government of Indias Vision of Amrit Kaal from 2022 to 2047, the Indian Auto Industry continues to face several headwinds in terms of geo-political uncertainties, supply side challenges, high commodity prices, high interest rates, increase in fuel prices and low market demand in some of the segments.

  • SIAM 1st MASTER CLASS Expert talk by Mr Brian West on US experience on usage of Higher Blends of Ethanol in Transportation

    Venue : , Virtual Platform Date : 26-Aug-2022

    Govt of India has mandated a joint Ministry of Heavy Industries (MHI) - SIAM Ethanol Awareness Campaign for Promotion of Ethanol as Transportation Fuel. Indias ethanol blending programme is being monitored at the highest level of Government and PMO is directly monitoring the progress. The important deadlines, which were decided for pan India implementation of E-20 are 2023 for vehicle to be material compliant and 2025 for vehicles to be fully E-20 compliant. Recently SIAM commemorated World Biofuels Day on 10th August 2022 by organizing a Seminar titled Promotion of Biofuels: A Pathway for Low Carbon Mobility as part of the year-round activities.

  • SIAM -IMT Ghaziabad Joint Workshop on INDUSTRY 4.0: Driving Towards Digital Transformation across Organizations

    Venue : , Virtual Date : 24-Aug-2022

    SIAM continuously works towards supporting sustainable development of the Indian Automobile Industry and facilitates enhancement of its competitiveness. SIAM also regularly engages in public policy advocacy and organizes innovative programs with stakeholders of the automotive industry. Towards this, SIAM has created a specific group namely Manufacturing, Industry 4.0 and Ease of Doing Business, with a focus on the upcoming business innovations and practices. The group organized an international lecture in July 2021 entitled Digital Innovation and Smart Manufacturing: Industry 4.0 and Beyond, which was represented by a host of International and National speakers and was well received with participation of over 400 delegates virtually.
